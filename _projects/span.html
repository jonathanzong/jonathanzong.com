SPAN 2017 is a conference about design and technology, hosted by Google. As an intern at Material UX and Google Design, I had the opportunity to help work on the website, design print assets for the event, and develop a redesigned beginner-friendly codelab which I then ran workshop sessions for on the day of the event.

The livestream viewing kit I worked on (below) helped people who couldn't attend the conference learn how to watch the livestream. The viewing kit page was promoted on Google Design's social media.
